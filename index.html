Tool Coverage
What Milo answers across the ecommerce stack.
Every item below is a question the team can ask in plain English and get answered on the spot.
🧲

CRM

Salesforce · HubSpot
  • Pipeline health: real-time deal-stage progression, win rates, velocity by rep/team
  • Lead scoring: which sources convert best, time-to-close by segment
  • Lifecycle reporting: cohort retention, repeat purchase rate, LTV by acquisition channel
  • Forecasting: weighted pipeline vs. actuals, quota attainment tracking
  • Hygiene alerts: stale deals, missing fields, duplicate detection
✉️

Email & Retention

Klaviyo
  • Flow performance: revenue per automation (welcome, abandoned cart, win-back), A/B results
  • List health: engagement decay, unsubscribe trends, deliverability monitoring
  • Segment deep-dives: RFM cohort revenue, cross-sell opportunity sizing
  • Campaign ROI: revenue per send, optimal send-frequency analysis
📣

Paid Social

Facebook · LinkedIn · TikTok
  • Cross-platform ROAS: unified spend vs. revenue across all paid channels
  • Creative fatigue: CTR/CPA trends by ad creative over time
  • Audience performance: which lookalikes / interests / demographics convert profitably
  • Budget allocation: diminishing-returns analysis, spend-rebalancing recommendations
▶️

Organic Social

YouTube · Instagram · TikTok
  • Content performance: engagement rate, saves, shares by content type/format
  • Posting cadence: optimal frequency and timing per platform
  • Follower attribution: which content drives follows vs. just impressions
  • Funnel contribution: organic posts correlated with sessions and conversions
🔎

Web & SEO

GA4 · Tag Manager · Search Console · Google Ads
  • Full-funnel: session → add-to-cart → purchase conversion by channel/campaign
  • SEO visibility: keyword movement, CTR by page, cannibalisation detection
  • Landing pages: bounce, scroll depth, CTA click-through by traffic source
  • Paid search efficiency: quality score trends, wasted spend, SKAG analysis
  • Attribution: multi-touch paths across paid + organic + email
🤖

Delivery & Access

Browser · Slack · WhatsApp · Teams
  • Ask anywhere: same answer quality on the channel the team already uses
  • Scheduled digests: automated Slack/email packs, no manual exports
  • Proactive alerts: Milo flags the moment a metric moves, before you ask
  • Unlimited users: the whole team self-serves, not just one analyst

Cross-Source: the real power

Every source above, joined together
  • Blended CAC & LTV: Meta / Google / TikTok spend combined with CRM revenue for true unit economics
  • Marketing → sales handoff: HubSpot / Salesforce lead journey mapped back to first-touch ad or content
  • Automated reporting: scheduled digests pulling from all sources, zero manual exports
  • Natural-language Q&A: anyone asks in plain English, gets an answer from any connected source instantly
